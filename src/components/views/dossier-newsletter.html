Market Analysis

  • Healthcare News Lag: Early-stage health and biotech financings are consistently reported with a 2-3 day lag compared to general tech news.
  • AI Infrastructure Dominance: The largest rounds reported today are flowing into core AI infrastructure and agent layers (e.g., Wonderful's $100M Series A), indicating a focus on foundational technologies.
  • Workflow Augmentation Theme: A clear pattern across all sectors is the funding of tools that augment or automate human workflows (nursing, legal, insurance, HR) rather than attempting outright replacement.
