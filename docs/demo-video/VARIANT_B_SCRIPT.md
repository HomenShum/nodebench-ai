## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Why this works (Notion/Superhuman pattern)
- **Emotional arc**: Pain (chaos) -> Clarity (reframe) -> Competence (product) -> Confidence (proof). The viewer's emotional state mirrors the founder's journey.
- **Relatability**: Every founder has had the "47 Slack messages at 7 AM" morning. The pain is universal.
- **Show the loop**: The video demonstrates the complete daily cycle — check, decide, dispatch, verify. The viewer understands HOW they'd use it, not just what it does.
- **Chief of staff framing**: "Delegate like you would to a chief of staff" is the positioning hook. It reframes AI agents from "tools" to "team."
- **The 4-minute morning**: Concrete, measurable, aspirational. "Your morning took four minutes" is the memorable line.

### Key difference from original
The original walks through features sequentially. This variant tells a STORY — the viewer experiences a founder's morning from chaos to clarity. The features are shown in service of the narrative, not as a feature tour. The emotional contrast between the gray chaos montage and the warm, organized dashboard is the hook that makes viewers feel something.
